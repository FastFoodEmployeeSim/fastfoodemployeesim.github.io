About

Fast Food Employee Simulator (FFES) is a game made by dev team Plum Studios that consisted of 3 people: Plumboy, Reddstone35, and DarthLazerBoo. The idea of the game is to run around taking customers orders, making it ingame, and completeing other various tasks. You can also collect money and unlock different things to add to the restaurant you were hired at. There is also different gamemodes, like endless, where you have to survive as much time as you can without getting fired, this gamemode gradually gets harder and harder. its purpose it to collect more money that you might not beable to get ingame.
